Guidelines and Resources

What is COVID-19

COVID-19 is a disease caused by the novel corona virus. Common symptoms are Fever, Dry cough, Breathing difficulty. Some patients also have aches and pains, nasal congestion, runny nose, sore throat or diarrhoea.

Know How it Spreads

The best way to prevent illness is to avoid being exposed to this virus. It spreads through contact with a sick person who when sneezes or coughs can produce tiny infected droplets which can land in the mouths or noses of people who are nearby or possibly be inhaled into the lungs.

How to Avoid Getting infected

→ STAY home
→ KEEP a safe distance
→ WASH hands often
→ COVER your cough
→ SICK? Call the helpline
